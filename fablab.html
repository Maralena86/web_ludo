FABLAB

Mechanic workshop

These are the machines I use when I make mechanical parts (aluminum, teflon, stainless steel ...). Milling is a precision work, I use it to make mirror supports, chambers, plates ... Turning is a machining process by removing material from a blank, the starting volume in which the part to be machined is to be shaped. The rotating part is eroded with a cutting tool in translation, this allows me to make cylinders or rings with threading adapted for the objectivesThe column allowed me to make holes and taps. The sandblasting machine allows me to make the surfaces less shinyTranslated

3D printing

I regularly use FDM to make ABS parts for turntables or benchtop supports. I use resin to make chambers for Spim, to make PDMS molds ... Finally the laser engraver allows me to make plastic cuttings or engravings.
